Sales Performance Dashboards (Tableau)

Built a pair of Tableau dashboards that let sales managers and executives track KPIs, uncover trends, and compare product sub‑categories — all refreshed automatically from the data warehouse.

Tableau KPI Tiles Sales Trends Product Comparison Weekly Heat Map

Business Problem

The sales leadership team needed real‑time visibility into revenue, profit, and quantity sold — broken out by month, week, and product sub‑category — to make faster merchandising and budgeting decisions.

Approach

  • Gathered requirements and translated them into four key dashboard sections: KPI Overview, Sales Trends, Product Sub‑Category Comparison, and Weekly Performance.
  • Modelled the data in Snowflake and exposed a clean star schema to Tableau for self‑service refreshes.
  • Designed consistent colour scales and tooltips so users can spot anomalies at a glance.

Key Requirements & Solutions

  • KPI Overview — Dynamic tiles compare current‑year and prior‑year totals with colour‑coded arrows.
  • Sales Trends — Dual‑year overlay line chart plus auto‑highlight of best & worst months.
  • Product Sub‑Category Comparison — Butterfly bar chart juxtaposes sales and profit; tooltips reveal margin %.
  • Weekly Performance Heat‑Map — Calendar view benchmarks each week against the YTD average.
