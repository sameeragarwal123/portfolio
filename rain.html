Mumbai Temperature & Rain-Prediction

Built next-day forecasts for temperature (robust regression, R² 0.84) and rain probability (neural-network classifier, 90 % accuracy) using eight years of daily Mumbai weather data—insights that let tourism and logistics firms plan staff, inventory and energy usage.

Neural Network Robust Regression Time-Series Weather Data Lag Features

Business Problem

Mumbai’s volatile monsoon disrupts transport, retail demand and energy usage. Accurate next-day predictions of temperature and rainfall help companies optimise staffing, logistics and advertising spend.

Approach

  • Ingested eight-year daily series: temperature, humidity, pressure, wind speed, precipitation flag.
  • Created one- and two-day lags for all predictors; encoded rain Yes/No to 1/0.
  • Temperature: stepwise robust OLS on lagged features (R² 0.84).
  • Rain: 7-feature neural net (2 hidden layers, 20 epochs) with min-max scaling; evaluated accuracy, sensitivity, specificity.

Key Results

  • Regression explains 83.8 % of temperature variance with 10 significant lagged predictors.
  • Neural-network rain model: 90 % accuracy, 87 % sensitivity, 92 % specificity.
  • Dashboard delivers probability of rain + temperature forecast to partners 24 h in advance.

Tech & Tools

Python · pandas · statsmodels · Keras · scikit-learn · matplotlib
