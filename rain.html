Mumbai Temperature &
Rain-Prediction Models

Forecasted daily temperature and probability of rainfall using eight years of Mumbai weather data. Combined Rob­ust Regression (R² 0.838) for temperature and a Neural-Network classifier (90 % accuracy) for precipitation—insights that guide logistics, tourism and energy planning.

Lag Regression Neural Network Time-Series Binary Classifier Standardization

Business Problem

Weather swings in Mumbai disrupt transport, retail demand and energy usage. Accurate next-day forecasts of temperature and rain probability help firms optimise staffing, inventory and advertising spend.

Approach

  • Daily dataset: temperature, dew point, humidity, sea-level pressure, wind speed/direction & precipitation flag.
  • Encoded precipitation (Yes / No) → 1 / 0; created 1- and 2-day lags for all predictors.
  • Temperature: stepwise robust OLS on lagged features (R² 0.838).
  • Rain: 7-feature neural net (2 hidden layers, 20 epochs) using min-max scaling; evaluated with accuracy, sensitivity, specificity, PPV, NPV.

Key Results

  • Regression explains 83.8 % of temperature variance using 10 significant lagged predictors.
  • Neural-network rain model: 90 % accuracy, 87 % sensitivity, 92 % specificity.
  • Dashboard gives probability of rain + temp forecast to hotel and tourism partners 24 h ahead.

Tech & Tools

Python · pandas · statsmodels · Keras · scikit-learn · matplotlib
