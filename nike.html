Project Details

Nike Customer Retention & Commitment Analysis

Conducted a deep-dive consumer-insights study for Nike, collecting primary data through a Qualtrics survey and complementary qualitative interviews. The analysis segmented customers, pinpointed the factors that encourage repeat purchases on Nike’s D2C channel, and recommended a tiered subscription model to strengthen retention.

Clustering Regression Customer Segmentation Qualitative Analysis Quantitative Analysis Survey

Business Problem

Nike wanted to understand the loyalty drivers behind its e-commerce customers and quantify the impact of a membership-based retention program.

Approach

  • Designed a 1.5 K-response Qualtrics survey plus in-depth interviews.
  • Performed K-means clustering on behavioural & attitudinal variables.
  • Built logistic & linear models to link cluster membership to repurchase probability.

Key Results

  • Identified three high-value segments representing 48 % of revenue.
  • Proposed a tiered subscription lifting retention by 12 %.
  • Projected incremental revenue of $2 M / year at 80 % adoption.

Tech & Tools

Python · scikit-learn · pandas · Qualtrics · Tableau
