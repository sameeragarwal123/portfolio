Project Details

Nike Customer Retention & Commitment Analysis

Deep-dive consulting project identifying key factors that drive repeat purchases for Nike’s D2C channel and proposing a tiered subscription model.

Clustering Regression CX

Business Problem

Nike wanted to understand the loyalty drivers behind its e-commerce customers and quantify the impact of a membership-based retention program.

Approach

  • Designed a 1.5K-response Qualtrics survey plus in-depth interviews.
  • Performed K-means clustering on behavioural & attitudinal variables.
  • Built logistic & linear models to link cluster membership to repurchase probability.

Key Results

  • Identified three high-value segments representing 48 % of revenue.
  • Proposed a tiered subscription that lifts retention by 12 %.
  • Projected incremental revenue of $2 M / year at 80 % adoption.
