Project Details

Prediction of Futures Commodity Prices

Developed a time-series model that forecasts next-day settlement prices for oil and gold futures. The pipeline engineered financial KPIs—trade volume, VWAP, liquidity ratios—to capture price dynamics and supply actionable signals for high-frequency trading desks and commodity hedge funds.

Time Series Data Pre-Processing Predictive Modeling Variation Inflation Factor Finance Ordinary Least Squares

Business Problem

Commodity desks rely on heuristics and subjective views, leading to missed entry points and excess exposure. A robust, data-driven forecast improves position sizing and hedging.

Approach

  • Ingested 1.5 million daily contracts for crude oil and gold (CME).
  • Engineered technical indicators: rolling volatility, momentum, term-structure spread, volume spikes.
  • Benchmarked ARIMA, Prophet, Gradient Boosting; Random Forest delivered best out-of-sample MAE.
  • Performed walk-forward validation with expanding window to mimic live trading.

Key Results

  • Random Forest reduced forecast error by 11 % versus random-walk baseline.
  • Simulated strategy improved Sharpe ratio from 0.78 to 1.06.
  • Risk team adopted model to set dynamic VaR limits, cutting tail-risk exposure 9 %.

Tech & Tools

Python · pandas · scikit-learn · statsmodels · Plotly
